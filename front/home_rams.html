RAMS

RAMS(Resource Access and Management Software) is a tool which helps teams and individuals share important files and resources. At any point of time while pondering over a website, you find something interesting and you want to let someone or your whole team know that it's an important link, you add it to the software and the software will make sure to share the link across the team or with select few individuals.
